    About

    My name is Rian Ferreira, a tech enthusiast with over 11 years of hands-on experience with computers—from hardware tinkering to software development. In March 2024, I decided to turn this passion into a career and started my degree in Systems Analysis and Development (ADS), where I’ve been focusing on: Back-end & Desktop: Developing with C# and .NET to build robust, scalable solutions. My goal is to combine clean logic (thank you, C#!) and data organization (SQL to the rescue) to create applications that solve real-world problems.
